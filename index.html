ABOUT OUR ACADEMY

RJ'GenNext'Academy is dedicated to cultivating not just martial artists, but disciplined and confident individuals. We offer traditional and modern martial arts training under highly certified instructors, focusing on physical fitness, mental resilience, and character development.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to instill **discipline, respect, and confidence** in every student, providing them with the tools to succeed both on and off the mat. We are committed to excellence in all aspects of martial arts training.

PROGRAMS AND CLASSES

Karate is a powerful, empty-hand discipline promoting discipline and precision. Our classes focus on traditional Okinawan Karate, including **Kata (forms), Kumite (sparring), and Tameshiwari (breaking)**, suitable for all skill levels from beginner to black belt.

PENCAK SILAT CLASS

Master the art of this dynamic Indonesian martial art. Pencak Silat training emphasizes fluid movements, strikes, joint manipulation, and traditional weapon use. It's a comprehensive self-defense system recognized globally.

Pencak Silat is a traditional martial art with roots in the Indonesian/Malay archipelago, including Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, and Brunei. The term "pencak silat" was chosen in 1948 as a unifying term for the Indonesian fighting styles. It encompasses a variety of styles depending on the region and ethnic groups where each style originated.
